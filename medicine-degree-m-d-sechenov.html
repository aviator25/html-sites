MEDICINE DEGREE (M.D.) (SECHENOV)

PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

Duration: 6 Years – Total Cost:RM308,000($77000)
**including Tuition Fees, Hostel fees and Insurance fees.

PROGRAMME STRUCTURE & COURSES

The program of Doctor of Medicine is an educational program develop practical and clinical capabilities in maintain and restore health through the prevention and treatment of illness.

In order to improve the quality of academic and sharpen its professional skill, the universities have cooperated with many agencies and professionals engaged in health care service, for example hospitals, health centers, polyclinics, independent midwives and other health care services at local, regional and national level.

The universities are located in a campus with a nice atmosphere, creating a positive climate that is conducive to student learning. These Government universities are provided with various facilities that support education, research and community service are also recognized by our Malaysian Public Service Department (JPA) and Malaysian Medical Council (MMC).

The program aims to educate students by applying teaching-learning processes that compels the completion of a curriculum package in order that students obtain sufficient knowledge, skills and behavior. Moreover, the curriculum model used is competence-based education.

This model requires integration of scientific disciplines and a multidisciplinary approach in curriculum delivery. A balance between specific and general courses is obtained, in each subject as the learning outcomes are mixture of generic and specific competencies.

MINIMUM ENTRY REQUIREMENT

Foundation In Science/STPM/Diploma/South Australia Matriculation(SAM)/Canadian Pre-U (CPU)/UEC/A-Levels/And other equivalent qualification.
