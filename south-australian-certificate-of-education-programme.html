CANADIAN INTERNATIONAL MATRICULATION PROGRAMME/ CANADIAN PRE-UNIVERSITY PROGRAMME

PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

YATHIS recommends the South Australian Certificate of Education (SACE International) programme which is your gateway to university studies worldwide. It provides the pathway to a broad spectrum of undergraduate studies including medicine, pharmacy, dentistry, engineering, biotechnology, computer science, law, business, mass communications and other programmes.
